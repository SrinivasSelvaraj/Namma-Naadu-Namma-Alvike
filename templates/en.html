About Us

Founded on the principle of "Our Land, Our Identity", the Karnataka-Centric Party is dedicated to protecting the rights, culture, language, and future of Kannadigas.

Our Objectives:

  • Prioritize Kannada language and culture
  • Equitable development of Karnataka
  • Youth empowerment and women's empowerment
  • Clean and ethical politics
  • Commitment to the welfare of Kannadigas
